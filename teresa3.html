Reduce

In order to save the environment, we should reduce our usage of plastic bags and other resources to avoid waste.









Reuse

We should reuse unwanted materials, or donate them to others in need. For example, using reusable water bottles instead of plastic bottles.















Recycle

We should throw recyclable materials into the recycling bin. By recycling, we will be able to transform old materials into a new item.




Start reducing, reusing and recycling today!!!!
